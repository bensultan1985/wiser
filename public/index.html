Our Mission:

At Wiser, we believe the key to growth is learning from ourselves and others. Perhaps buying your first car taught you an important lesson. Perhaps you found the secret to maintaining a loving marriage. We want to hear it! Or, if you'd like to share a famous motto that you live by, we want to know! Together, our community will spotlight the most user-tested, success-achieving wisdom.

Commenting Guidelines:

Comments should be courteous and well-thought-out. A wisbit is ranked by the amount of favorites and comments it receives. Therefore, if you disagree with the wisdom, your best course of action is to create your own alternative wisbit, which may gain traction and win in the battle of ideas!

Welcome to the Wishing Well!

Although this is an intellect-driven site, even the most secular and analytic of us need a wish now and then.

Release your inner wants and desires, anonymously. Each day, you will gain one "wish penny" to toss into our waterless well. The neat part is, by grabbing at the well, you can read any wish penny in the history of the well. This well is never emptied!
